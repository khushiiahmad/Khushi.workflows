AI Course Enrollment Automation System (n8n)

This automation system is designed for education institutes, business coaches, and digital course creators who want to automate their student enrollment process.

The system handles lead capture, follow-ups, payment processing, and meeting scheduling automatically, reducing manual work for the team.

The automation is divided into three workflows.

Workflow 1 – Lead Capture & Confirmation

When a student fills out a form on the website:

The lead information is automatically saved in Google Sheets.

A confirmation email is sent to the student asking whether they want to enroll in the course.

The system waits for the student’s response.

This ensures every lead is captured and responded to automatically.

Workflow 2 – Student Response Processing

When the student replies to the email, the system analyzes the response.

If the student wants to enroll:

The system calculates the course fee automatically.

A personalized invoice and payment link is sent to the student via email.

If the payment is not completed, a reminder is sent after 24 hours.

Once payment is completed, the owner receives a WhatsApp alert with the student’s details.

This helps automate the entire payment and enrollment process.

If the student is not ready to enroll:

The student receives an email asking for their reason.

The email includes a Calendly link where they can book a meeting with the educator.

Only the available time slots of the educator are shown.

Once a meeting is booked, the owner receives a WhatsApp notification.

This helps convert hesitant leads into potential students.

Workflow 3 – Meeting Notification & Lead Summary

When a meeting is booked through Calendly:

The system sends a WhatsApp alert to the owner.

The message includes:

Student details

Their responses from previous emails

A summary of the conversation

This allows the owner to quickly understand the student's situation without going through multiple emails before the meeting.

Additional Features

• Every email includes the team contact number and WhatsApp support
• Automatic lead tracking using Google Sheets
• Smart follow-up reminders
• WhatsApp notifications for important updates
